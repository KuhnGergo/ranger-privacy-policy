Privacy Policy - Ranger

Last updated: March 29, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how our application collects, uses, and protects your information.

📷 Camera Usage

Our application may request access to your device camera only for the purpose of creating a profile picture.

The camera is used only when you explicitly choose to take a profile photo.
The application does not access the camera in the background.
You are not required to upload a profile picture to use the app.
🖼️ Image Storage

If you choose to upload a profile picture:

The image will be stored on our secure servers.
The image is associated only with your user account.
You can delete your profile picture at any time from within the app.
🔒 Data Usage

We only use your data to:

Provide app functionality
Store and display your profile image

We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

🧹 Data Deletion

You have full control over your data:

You can remove your profile image at any time.
Once deleted, the image is permanently removed from our servers.
📡 Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data from unauthorized access.
